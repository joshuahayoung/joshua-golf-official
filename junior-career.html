Junior Career

Between 2019 and 2025, Hong competed in tournaments organized by the Mexican Golf Federation (FMG), the Mexico Junior Golf Tour (MJGT), and events valid for the World Amateur Golf Ranking (WAGR). He represented Mexico in international junior competitions and participated in open tournaments in South America and the United States. His junior career is documented through official results published by recognized federations and circuits.
